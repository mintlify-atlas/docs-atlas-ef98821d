The Sleep view provides detailed analysis of your sleep quality, duration, and composition based on WHOOP's sleep tracking.

## Sleep Performance Score

The sleep performance score (0-100%) represents how well you slept relative to your body's needs. It considers:

- Hours slept vs. hours needed
- Sleep consistency
- Sleep efficiency
- Sleep stages (light, deep, REM)
- Disturbances and wake time

## Key Metrics

The Sleep detail view displays several key metrics with color-coded progress bars:

### Sleep Performance

- **Display**: Percentage (0-100%)
- **Progress Bar**: 3-segment bar with thresholds at 70% and 85%
- **Colors**: Orange (poor), gray (sufficient), teal (optimal)

### Hours vs. Needed

Shows how much sleep you got compared to your personalized sleep need.

- **Display**: Percentage (0-100%)
- **Calculation**: `(hours_slept / hours_needed) * 100`
- **Capped**: Maximum of 100%
- **Progress Bar**: 3-segment bar with thresholds at 70% and 85%

<Info>
Your sleep need is dynamic and adjusts based on recent strain, sleep debt, baseline needs, and recent naps.
</Info>

### Sleep Consistency

Measures how consistent your sleep schedule is.

- **Display**: Percentage (0-100%)
- **Progress Bar**: 3-segment bar with thresholds at 70% and 80%
- **Higher is better**: Consistent sleep times improve recovery

### Sleep Efficiency

Percentage of time in bed actually spent asleep.

- **Display**: Percentage (0-100%)
- **Calculation**: `(sleep_time / time_in_bed) * 100`
- **Progress Bar**: 3-segment bar with thresholds at 80% and 90%
- **Data Source**: `SleepScore.SleepEfficiencyPercentage`

### Sleep Stages Chart

Displays a horizontal bar chart showing:
- **Duration**: Time spent in each stage (hours:minutes)
- **Percentage**: Proportion of total sleep time
- **Baseline**: Gray reference line showing your 7-day average sleep duration
- **Colors**: Stage-specific colors (awake=gray, light=indigo, deep=purple, REM=pink)

<Note>
The percentage calculations exclude awake time from the denominator to show the composition of actual sleep.
</Note>

## Sleep Need Breakdown

A detailed visualization of why your body needs a certain amount of sleep:

### Components

1. **Hours of Sleep** (actual)
   - Time in bed minus time awake
   - Displayed as horizontal bar

2. **Sleep Needed** (target)
   - Stacked bar showing three components:
     - **Healthy Minimum** (teal): Your baseline sleep need
     - **Recent Strain** (orange): Additional need from physical activity
     - **Sleep Debt** (red): Accumulated deficit from previous nights

### Sleep Need Formula

```
Total Need = Baseline + Strain Need + Sleep Debt - Recent Naps
```

- **Baseline**: Your personal healthy minimum (typically 7-9 hours)
- **Strain Need**: Added by intense workouts and high strain days
- **Sleep Debt**: Builds when you don't meet your need
- **Naps**: Deducted from total need (shown as negative/signed value)

<Info>
The sleep need visualization uses signed durations (+/-) to clearly show additions and deductions.
</Info>

## Restorative Sleep

Combined deep (SWS) and REM sleep - the most restorative sleep stages.

- **Display**: Total duration (hours:minutes)
- **Direction Indicator**: Compared to 7-day average
  - ↑ (teal) when above average
  - ↓ (orange) when below average
- **Symbol**: Split-colored square (◤) representing REM (top) and SWS (bottom)

## Weekly Trends

Scroll down to see charts showing 7-day trends:

### Weekly Sleep Performance
- **Chart Type**: Color-coded bar chart
- **Colors**: Same as sleep score (orange/gray/teal)
- **Data**: Last 7 sleep sessions (excluding naps)

### Hours of Sleep vs. Needed
- **Chart Type**: Dual-line chart
- **Lines**: 
  - Purple: Hours of sleep (actual)
  - Teal: Sleep needed (target)
- **Legend**: Top-right corner
- **Y-Axis**: Auto-scaled to fit data

### Hours vs. Needed (%)
- **Chart Type**: Bar chart
- **Data**: Percentage of sleep need met
- **Y-Axis**: 0-100%

### Restorative Sleep
- **Chart Type**: Stacked bar chart
- **Segments**:
  - Pink: REM sleep
  - Purple: Deep sleep (SWS)
- **Legend**: Shows both stages with reverse ordering

### Sleep Consistency (%)
- **Chart Type**: Bar chart
- **Data**: Consistency percentage over 7 days

### Sleep Efficiency (%)
- **Chart Type**: Line chart with dots
- **Color**: Purple
- **Data**: Efficiency percentage over 7 days

### Sleep Debt
- **Chart Type**: Bar chart
- **Data**: Sleep debt in hours
- **Y-Axis**: Max of 3 hours
- **Color**: Purple

<Note>
All sleep charts automatically exclude naps and filter data to the selected date when viewing historical records.
</Note>

## Navigation

While in the Sleep detail view:
- **←/h or →/l**: Switch to Recovery or Strain tabs
- **↑/↓ or j/k**: Scroll through metrics and charts
- **Mouse Wheel**: Scroll content
- **esc**: Return to Overview
- **[/]**: Navigate to previous/next day's sleep data

## Data Sources

Sleep data comes from:
- **Primary**: `/v2/cycle/{id}/sleep` - Sleep associated with the current cycle
- **Historical**: `/v2/activity/sleep` - Last 7 days for trend charts

### Sleep Data Structure

Key fields from `internal/client/whoop/types.go`:

```go
type Sleep struct {
    ID         string
    CycleID    int64
    Start      time.Time
    End        time.Time
    Nap        bool      // Excluded from charts
    Score      *SleepScore
}

type SleepScore struct {
    StageSummary               SleepStages
    SleepNeeded                SleepNeeded
    SleepPerformancePercentage float64
    SleepConsistencyPercentage float64
    SleepEfficiencyPercentage  float64
    RespiratoryRate            float64
}
```

## Data Availability

If sleep data is not available:
- Gauge shows `--`
- Metrics section displays "No sleep data available"
- Charts are hidden

This occurs when:
- Viewing a day without sleep data
- Sleep hasn't been scored yet (takes ~30min after waking)
- Viewing future dates
