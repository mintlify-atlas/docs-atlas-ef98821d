The Recovery view provides in-depth analysis of your body's readiness to perform, based on WHOOP's physiological measurements during sleep.

## Recovery Score

The recovery score (0-100%) is WHOOP's primary metric for determining how prepared your body is for strain. It's calculated from:

- Heart Rate Variability (HRV)
- Resting Heart Rate (RHR)
- Respiratory Rate
- Sleep Performance
- Skin Temperature
- Blood Oxygen Saturation (SpO2)

Recovery scores are color-coded to indicate status:

## Key Metrics

The Recovery detail view displays the following metrics with 30-day average comparisons:

### HRV (Heart Rate Variability)

**Higher is better** - Measures the variation in time between heartbeats.

- **Display**: Rounded to nearest millisecond (e.g., "45")
- **Direction Indicator**: 
  - ↑ (teal) when above 30-day average
  - ↓ (orange) when below 30-day average
- **Data Source**: `RecoveryScore.HRVRmssdMilli` (internal/client/whoop/types.go:51)

### Resting Heart Rate

**Lower is better** - Your heart rate during rest/sleep.

- **Display**: Rounded to nearest BPM (e.g., "52")
- **Direction Indicator**: 
  - ↓ (teal) when below 30-day average (good)
  - ↑ (orange) when above 30-day average (bad)
- **Data Source**: `RecoveryScore.RestingHeartRate` (internal/client/whoop/types.go:50)

### Respiratory Rate

**Lower is better** - Breaths per minute during sleep.

- **Display**: One decimal place (e.g., "16.2")
- **Direction Indicator**: 
  - ↓ (teal) when below 30-day average (good)
  - ↑ (orange) when above 30-day average (bad)
- **Data Source**: `SleepScore.RespiratoryRate` (internal/client/whoop/types.go:74)

<Note>
Respiratory rate comes from the associated sleep record, not the recovery record.
</Note>

### Sleep Performance

**Higher is better** - How well you slept compared to your needs.

- **Display**: Percentage rounded to nearest integer (e.g., "87%")
- **Direction Indicator**: 
  - ↑ (teal) when above 30-day average
  - ↓ (orange) when below 30-day average
- **Data Source**: `SleepScore.SleepPerformancePercentage` (internal/client/whoop/types.go:75)

## 30-Day Averages

Each metric shows a comparison to your 30-day average:

- **Average Value**: Displayed in gray below the current value
- **Direction Arrow**: Color-coded arrow indicating whether you're above or below average
- **Calculation**: Computed from the last 30 days of historical data (internal/tui/page/dashboard/commands.go)

<Info>
The direction arrows account for whether "higher is better" or "lower is better" for each metric, so teal always means good and orange always means concerning.
</Info>

## Weekly Trends

Scroll down to see charts showing 7-day trends for:

### Recovery Trend
- **Chart Type**: Bar chart with color-coded bars (green/yellow/red)
- **Data**: Recovery scores from the last 7 days
- **Y-Axis**: 0-100%

### HRV Trend
- **Chart Type**: Line chart with dots
- **Color**: Recovery blue
- **Data**: HRV values from last 7 recoveries

### Resting Heart Rate
- **Chart Type**: Line chart with dots
- **Color**: Recovery blue
- **Data**: RHR from last 7 recoveries

### Respiratory Rate
- **Chart Type**: Line chart with dots
- **Color**: Recovery blue
- **Data**: Respiratory rate from last 7 sleep sessions (excluding naps)

### Sleep Performance
- **Chart Type**: Bar chart with color-coded bars
- **Data**: Sleep performance % from last 7 sleep sessions
- **Y-Axis**: 0-100%

<Note>
Charts automatically filter out naps and only show data up to the currently selected date when viewing historical data.
</Note>

## Navigation

While in the Recovery detail view:
- **←/h or →/l**: Switch to Sleep or Strain tabs
- **↑/↓ or j/k**: Scroll through metrics and charts
- **Mouse Wheel**: Scroll content
- **esc**: Return to Overview
- **[/]**: Navigate to previous/next day's recovery data

## Scrolling

The Recovery view uses a lazy-loaded scrollable list:
- Content is rendered on-demand as you scroll
- Charts are cached to avoid re-rendering
- Scroll offset resets when switching tabs or changing dates

## Data Availability

If recovery data is not available:
- Gauge shows `--`
- Metrics section displays "No recovery data available" in gray italic text
- Charts are hidden

This can occur when:
- Viewing a date without sleep data
- Recovery hasn't been calculated yet (appears after waking)
- Viewing future dates
