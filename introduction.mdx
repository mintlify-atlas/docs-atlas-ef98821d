## Welcome to thoop

thoop is a terminal user interface (TUI) for viewing and analyzing your WHOOP fitness data. Built with modern Go libraries and designed for developers who live in the terminal, thoop brings your health metrics where you work.

## What is thoop?

thoop connects directly to your WHOOP account and displays your fitness data in a beautiful, keyboard-driven terminal interface. View your recovery scores, sleep performance, daily strain, and workout details without leaving your command line.

## Core Metrics

thoop displays the three primary WHOOP metrics in an intuitive dashboard:

- **Recovery Score** (0-100%) - How ready your body is for strain
- **Sleep Performance** (0-100%) - Quality and duration of your sleep
- **Day Strain** (0-21) - Cardiovascular load from activities

Navigate between Overview, Sleep, Recovery, and Strain tabs to drill into detailed metrics, historical trends, and individual workout data.

## How It Works

1. **Authenticate** - thoop uses OAuth to securely connect to your WHOOP account
2. **Sync Data** - Your cycles, sleep, recovery, and workouts are cached locally
3. **Browse Offline** - View historical data even without internet
4. **Stay Updated** - Real-time notifications keep your dashboard current

<Note>
  thoop stores credentials in your system keyring (Keychain on macOS, Secret Service on Linux, Credential Manager on Windows) for maximum security.
</Note>

## Technology Stack

thoop is built with:

- **[Bubble Tea](https://github.com/charmbracelet/bubbletea)** - Terminal UI framework
- **[Lip Gloss](https://github.com/charmbracelet/lipgloss)** - Styling and layout
- **[Cobra](https://github.com/spf13/cobra)** - CLI command structure
- **SQLite** - Local data persistence
- **Go** - Performance and cross-platform support
